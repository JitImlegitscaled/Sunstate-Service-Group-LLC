What We Evaluate

Our wind mitigation inspection assesses key structural features that protect your home during hurricanes and severe storms.

🏠

Roof Shape & Geometry

Hip roofs offer better wind resistance than gable roofs. We document the exact geometry for maximum insurance credits.

🔩

Roof-to-Wall Attachment

We verify how the roof is connected to the walls — clips, single wraps, or double wraps — which significantly impacts your discount.

🛡️

Roof Deck Attachment

The method used to attach the roof deck (plywood/OSB) to the trusses — nail size, spacing, and type all affect your premium.

💧

Secondary Water Resistance

Sealed roof deck (SWR) provides a secondary barrier against water intrusion if shingles are blown off during a storm.

🔷

Opening Protection

Hurricane shutters, impact-rated windows, and door protection — all verified and documented for your insurer.

📄

OIR-B1-1802 Form

We complete the official Florida OIR-B1-1802 form accepted by all insurance carriers in the state. Submit directly to your agent.
