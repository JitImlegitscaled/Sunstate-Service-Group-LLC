Inspection Coverage

What's Included in Your Inspection

We examine every major system and component of the home to ensure you have complete knowledge of its condition.

Roof & Exterior

Roofing materials, gutters, downspouts, siding, trim, windows, doors, grading, and drainage around the foundation.

Electrical Systems

Main panel, sub-panels, wiring type, GFCI/AFCI protection, outlets, switches, and visible wiring condition throughout.

Plumbing

Water supply lines, drain lines, water heater, fixtures, faucets, toilets, and visible pipe material and condition.

HVAC & Ventilation

Heating and cooling equipment, ductwork, thermostats, air handler, condensate drains, and overall system performance.

Structure & Foundation

Foundation type, visible cracks, settlement, framing, load-bearing walls, and structural integrity of the building.

Interior & Insulation

Walls, ceilings, floors, doors, windows, insulation in accessible areas, ventilation, and signs of moisture or damage.
