2. Scope & Limitations of Inspections

Our inspections are visual, non-invasive examinations of the accessible areas of a property at the time of the inspection. Inspections are not technically exhaustive and do not include:

  • Areas that are concealed, inaccessible, or obstructed

  • Environmental hazards such as mold, asbestos, radon, or lead paint (unless specifically contracted)
